Vietnames Salad

Description

This cold vietnamese salad is made with sliced cold lamb and fresh rice noodles.

Tossed through a salad. Completed with dressing

Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • Rice noodles
  • Salad
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Slice lamb
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Add lamb to noodles and salad
  4. Add dressing
